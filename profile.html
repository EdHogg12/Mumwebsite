About the Artist

Jess Wallwork

Jess Wallwork is a contemporary artist whose work is a conversation between texture, color, and emotion. Based in Austin, Texas, she has spent over a decade developing a unique practice that transforms interior spaces into immersive, tactile experiences. Her large-scale wall paintings are more than just decoration; they are architectural elements that shape the mood and story of a room.

Drawing inspiration from organic forms, weathered surfaces, and the Japanese philosophy of Wabi-sabi, Jess embraces imperfection and transience in her work. She believes that a wall should not be a static backdrop, but a living canvas that evolves with the light and life around it.

My Philosophy

"For me, a wall is the soul of a room. It holds the light, absorbs the sounds, and witnesses the life that unfolds before it. My goal is to give that soul a voice. I use natural pigments, lime wash, and plaster to build up layers of texture, creating surfaces that invite touch and contemplation. Each project is a unique collaboration with the space and its inhabitants, a process of listening and responding to create something truly personal and enduring."
